LIZ'S FAREWELL GIFT: A MAGICAL... PEN??


Soon you will awaken from your slumber and you will forget about this place. A tinge of somber coldness tugs at the edges of your heart. You don't want to leave, but you can't stay here forever as there are many who love you and need you in their lives. You cannot pinpoint what it is exactly that is so enchanting about this place. Was it the strange Lizard king or whatever? Or the warm aura of this place? As you lose yourself in thought, a familiar voice chimes in from behind you.

➤ "Fear not our farewell friend! Take this pen and write to me when you return to your realm!"
She hands you an old fashioned ink pen. It is so old fashioned that it has a good ol' feather attached to it.

➤ "Good luck on your journey, I hope to see you again!"
