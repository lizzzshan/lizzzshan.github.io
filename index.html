I am a recent grad from University of Colorado Boulder with a BA in computer science and a minor in economics. I have the most experience in software, though I also love design and UI/UX.

Aside from art, my hobbies include nature & gardening. I maintain a mini greenhouse with too many houseplants to count, & a small worm bin.

Photograph creds to URAMESHI

I am always looking for more ways to be more sustainable. One of the most rewarding things I do is vermicompost, which is basically composting but with worms. I give worms my food & paper scraps, & in return they give me the best kind of fertilizer: worm castings. I prevent my food waste from going into the landfill, & my worms give me fertilizer for my plants. It's a win-win!

I also love my dogs Ranger & Missy! During the summer I am almost always at the dog park with them.
