My team and I created an educational website that aims to teach kids about astronomy while raising awareness about climate change. Our idea for the website came to life through our diverse interests. This was everyone's first Hackathon experience, and I would say our greatest achievements are learning how to tackle challenges in code languages we are not familiar with.

My contributions include: drawing the icons, images, and planets through Procreate. Implementing the map view and multiple modals through the D3 package, and contributing to css, JS, and html changes with the team.
