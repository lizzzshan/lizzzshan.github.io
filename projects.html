The Planeteers - T9Hacks "outstanding beginner" award spring 2022

Hover over the images to preview or click on the image to go straight to the website! Preview remains open on mouseover.

The Planeteers is a beautiful & engaging kids' educational website with hand drawn graphics, brought to you by a team of first time hackers!
Front end was very new to us. So new to us that 3 of our 4 team members had to learn HTML on the spot! Despite having so little experience &
having such diverse knowledge amongst our teammates (2 computer science majors, 1 astrophysics major, & 1 math major), we came together to
create a website that merged all of our ideas. We challenged ourselves & attended many HTML workshops tha were offered to us within the 24
hours offered to us. I personally believe the best prize I won was this wonderful experience I got to share with my amazing team :)
