T9 Hackathon winner for "outstanding beginner" - DevPost link
My team and I worked on developing an interactive educational website that explores facts about space. The user is able to explore habital zones and the effects of space junk. I drew all the images on the website with Procreate, implemented interactive modals using D3, & added hover animations. I worked with the team to modify the css and research information to put on our website. Our accomplishment holds the title of outstanding beginner as this our team's very first Hackathon event.
